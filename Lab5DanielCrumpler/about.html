About Us

History

At Carrollton School of Gymnastics we have over 30 years experience teaching children in our community. We offer classes Monday – Thursday. Our programs are family oriented for you and your student-athlete.

We also offer compeitive teams for boys and girls. Team CSG follows the competitive level system set forth by USAG for both teams.
